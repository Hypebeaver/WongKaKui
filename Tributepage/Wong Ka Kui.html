Wong Ka Kui

Wong Ka Kui was born in British Hong Kong (1962). He is a huge fan of David Bowie and he taught himself to play guitar at age 15.

"Beyond" was created in year 1980 under a contest "Guitar Magazine". The band name was created by one of the member, William Tang (lead guitarist). It means as a transcend themself in aspect of attainment.

After releasing Goodbye Ideals (1986), EP Waiting Forever (1986), Arabian Dancing Girls (1987) and Modern Stage (1988), they get noticed by KINN's MUSIC LTD.

In year 1988, they signed contract with Cinepoly's and made album Secret Police (1988). Different album has been released in the following year such as The Grand Earth (1988), Truly Love You (1989), Destiny Party (1990) and Hesitation (1991)

Wong has signed a new contract with Amuse in Japan to continue the band career in 1990 which made the albums Continue the Revolution (1992) and Rock and Roll (1993)

Wong died at 1993 in Tokyo Women's Medical University Hospital that he fall off the platform during the band performance.
